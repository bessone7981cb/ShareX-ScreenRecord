## 📌 About ShareX

**ShareX** is a free and open-source program that lets you capture or record any area of your screen and share it with a single press of a key.  
It also supports uploading images, text files, and various other file types to over 80 supported destinations.

✅ Screenshot capture (full screen, region, window, etc.)  
✅ Screen recording (video & GIF)  
✅ OCR (text recognition)  
✅ Custom uploaders  
✅ Image editing & effects  
✅ Many hotkeys & workflows
